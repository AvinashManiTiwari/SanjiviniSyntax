What is SanjiviniSyntax?

SanjiviniSyntax is an online learning platform that provides programming courses, notes, quizzes and video tutorials.

How do I create an account?

Click on the Register button, fill in your details and create your free account.

Are the courses free?

We provide both free and premium learning resources depending on the course.

Can I download PDF notes?

Yes, semester-wise notes are available for download after login.

How do quizzes work?

Choose a quiz category, answer the questions and view your score instantly.

Will I receive a certificate?

Certificates are available after completing eligible courses and assessments.

How can I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to reset your password.

Can I access the website on mobile?

Yes, SanjiviniSyntax is fully responsive and works on desktops, tablets and smartphones.
